Conclusion

This project successfully extracts, visualizes, and analyzes financial data from an XML file. The following key insights were derived:

Direct Income Breakdown: Visualized via a bar chart to showcase different revenue streams.

Expense Distribution: Represented through a pie chart for better understanding of cost allocations.

Income vs. Expense Trend: A line chart illustrating financial performance over time.

Financial Ratios: Gross Profit Ratio, Net Profit Ratio, and Expense Ratio calculated to assess profitability.

Summary Tables: Top 5 income sources and expenses identified for a clear financial overview.

By automating financial data extraction and visualization, this project aids in better decision-making and performance tracking for financial analysis. The structured approach ensures clear insights, making it a useful tool for financial assessment.
